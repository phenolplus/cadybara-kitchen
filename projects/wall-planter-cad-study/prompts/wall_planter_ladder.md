# Wall Planter Prompt Ladder

These prompts intentionally describe the same object with increasing specificity.

## Prompt 1 - Minimal

Make me a planter I can put on my wall.

## Prompt 2 - Basic Form Intent

Design a wall-mounted planter that holds a small potted plant.

## Prompt 3 - Mounting Concept

Design a wall-mounted planter that holds a small potted plant and can be hung on a nail or screw in drywall.

## Prompt 4 - Rough Size

Design a wall-mounted planter about the size of a coffee mug, that holds a small potted plant and can be hung on a nail or screw in drywall.

## Prompt 5 - Shape And Orientation

Design a cylindrical wall-mounted planter about the size of a coffee mug, open at the top, that holds a small potted plant and hangs flat against the wall on a single screw.

## Prompt 6 - Approximate Dimensions

Design a cylindrical wall-mounted planter roughly 90 mm tall and 80 mm in diameter, open at the top, with a flat back that hangs against the wall using a single screw through a mounting hole.

## Prompt 7 - Wall Thickness And Material Context

Design a cylindrical 3D-printable wall-mounted planter, 90 mm tall and 80 mm outer diameter, with a 3 mm wall thickness, an open top, and a flat back panel containing one mounting hole sized for a #8 screw.

## Prompt 8 - Drainage And Functional Features

Design a cylindrical 3D-printable wall-mounted planter, 90 mm tall and 80 mm outer diameter, with a 3 mm wall thickness, an open top, a flat back panel, one keyhole-shaped mounting slot for a #8 screw centered on the back panel, and four 3 mm drainage holes in the bottom.

## Prompt 9 - Geometric Placement Of Features

Design a cylindrical 3D-printable wall-mounted planter. Outer diameter 80 mm, height 90 mm, wall thickness 3 mm. The back is a flat panel tangent to the cylinder, 100 mm tall x 90 mm wide, extending 5 mm above the cylinder top. Centered on the upper portion of the back panel, 15 mm from its top edge, is a keyhole-shaped mounting slot (8 mm circle on top, 4 mm wide x 10 mm tall slot below) sized for a #8 screw. The bottom of the cylinder has four 3 mm drainage holes arranged in a square pattern, each 15 mm from the central axis.

## Prompt 10 - Fully Specified

Design a 3D-printable wall-mounted planter with the following specifications. The body is a hollow cylinder, 80 mm outer diameter, 90 mm tall, with a uniform 3 mm wall thickness, open at the top and closed at the bottom. The bottom of the cylinder contains four 3 mm diameter drainage holes arranged in a square pattern, each centered 15 mm from the cylinder's central axis. The back is a flat rectangular panel, 100 mm tall x 90 mm wide x 4 mm thick, tangent to the outside of the cylinder along a vertical line, and extending 5 mm above the cylinder's top edge and 5 mm below its bottom edge. The back panel contains one keyhole-shaped mounting slot centered horizontally, with its top edge 15 mm below the top of the back panel: an 8 mm diameter circular opening with a 4 mm wide x 10 mm tall rectangular slot extending downward from the circle's center, sized to accept a #8 pan-head screw. All external edges except the open top rim have a 1 mm fillet. The model is a single watertight solid suitable for FDM 3D printing without supports when oriented with the back panel flat on the print bed.
